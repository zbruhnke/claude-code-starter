# Code of Conduct

## Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Assume positive intent
- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Inappropriate or unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

Report issues via the repository issue tracker. Violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

Maintainers will review reports promptly and fairly, respecting reporter privacy.
